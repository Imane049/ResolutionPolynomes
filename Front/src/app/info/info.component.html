What is a Polynomial?

A polynomial is a mathematical expression consisting of variables, coefficients, and exponents. Example: \( 3x^2 + 5x - 7 \).

Types of Polynomials

Polynomials can be classified based on their degree: linear, quadratic, cubic, and higher-order polynomials.
